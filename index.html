About Global Connect Logistics

Global Connect Logistics is a Kathmandu-based freight forwarder dedicated to connecting Nepal to the world through air, sea, and land transport. We ensure hassle-free cargo delivery with customs support, timely tracking, and reliable global partners.

  • 🔍 Shipment Tracking – Real-time visibility
